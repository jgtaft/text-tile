TEXT-TILE

Turn the text of your choice into a binary pattern (0=black, 1=white). Turn a poem, story, or any other words into a pattern for knitting, crochet, quilting, cross-stitch, or whatever strikes your fancy!

Tip: The result can get very long, so try a little text at a time.
